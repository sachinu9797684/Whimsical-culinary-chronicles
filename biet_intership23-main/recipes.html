Patoleo From Goa

The signature Goan sweet of Patoleo comprises of parboiled rice cooked with jaggery, coconut and a dash of cinnamon, followed by wrapping the steamed filling in turmeric leaves.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Mix rice flour, grated coconut, and jaggery to form a thick batter. Clean and cut banana leaves into square pieces.

step 2

Place a portion of the batter on each piece of banana leaf and fold to make a packet. Steam the packets for about 20-25 minutes

step 3

Allow them to cool before serving.

ingredients

2 cups rice flour

1 cup grated coconut

1 cup jaggery (grated)

Banana leaves for wrapping
